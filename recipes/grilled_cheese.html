Grilled Cheese Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices Bread
  • Butter
  • 3 Slices cheese
  • Sliced tomato
  • Dijon mustard

Steps

  1. Butter the outside of the bread
  2. Turn on the skillet
  3. Add slice of bread butter down, add cheese, add slice of bread butter up
  4. Brown bread
  5. Remove, open and add tomato slices and dijon
